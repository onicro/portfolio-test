How I Work

Process

Phase 01

Discovery & Research

Every project begins with deep listening. I research the brand, audience, competitors, and cultural context. This phase shapes the strategic foundation — understanding the "why" before exploring the "how."

Phase 02

Concept & Exploration

With strategy as the guide, I explore visual directions through mood boards, rough sketches, and typographic experiments. Multiple concepts are developed — each telling a different story.

Phase 03

Design & Refine

The chosen concept is refined with meticulous attention to detail — every pixel, curve, and color choice is deliberate. Iteration through feedback ensures the design evolves toward perfection.

Phase 04

Deliver & Launch

Final assets are delivered with comprehensive brand guidelines, ensuring consistent application across all touchpoints. I remain available through the launch phase and beyond.
